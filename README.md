# AI Interview Prep

An AI-powered interview preparation platform designed to help students and job seekers prepare for internships and placements through AI mock interviews, resume optimization, coding practice, aptitude training, and structured career roadmaps.

---

## Overview

AI Interview Prep is a comprehensive placement preparation platform built for engineering students and aspiring professionals. The platform combines interview preparation, coding practice, resume building, aptitude training, and personalized learning paths into a single ecosystem.

Whether you're preparing for software development, VLSI, core engineering roles, or campus placements, AI Interview Prep provides the tools and guidance needed to achieve your career goals.
